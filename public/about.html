About Us

At Precision Dataworks, we are committed to transforming the way organizations manage their data. Our EMSync (short for Employee Synchronization) database solution offers a comprehensive platform designed to streamline employee management processes for the College of Arts, Languages and Letters (CALL). EMSync reduces data discrepancies and ensures smooth synchronization across departments. The system also enhances operational efficiency, offering automated tracking for timesheets, payroll, employee training, and grievances. With a focus on user accessibility and security, EMSync empowers both administrators and employees, ensuring that all data remains accurate and easily accessible.

More About CALL

The College of Arts, Languages and Letters (CALL) at UH Mānoa focuses on the liberal arts with an emphasis on Hawai‘i, the Pacific, and Asia. It includes seventeen academic departments, the School of Pacific and Asian Studies, and various research centers. CALL offers a wide range of degree programs, from creative arts to language studies, and promotes interdisciplinary collaborations. The college's mission is to provide high-quality education and foster original research, with faculty leading in creative and scholarly work.
